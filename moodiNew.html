The Brief

"This was a concept I had for a mood-based music playlist generator. The emoji faces are clicked to generate playlists corresponding to mood, or dragged together to create custom mixed emotions."

Ideation

This originally began as a smarphone-concept, but the minimalism intrinsic in the design made me re-evaluate and create a sleek, simple design for Apple Watch.
