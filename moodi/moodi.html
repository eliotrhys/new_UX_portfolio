moodi

Mood-based music playlist generator (App Concept).

Click here to use the interactive prototype!

Note: This is not a functional app, just a design concept.

Also - the prototype colour scheme is slightly duller, this is a colour export problem with Adobe XD that is being fixed.


"moodi" was an idea I had in the first few weeks of CodeClan, during our first User Experience day. I thought the idea of generating playlists from moods would be great, but also gave me an idea for a left-field, creative interface idea.


The entire premise would be based on emojis: click happy for a happy playlist, sad for a sad one etc.

A more in-depth prototype would show the clicking-and-dragging of happy and sad to make melancholy, for example.
