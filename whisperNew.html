Mobile-first banking for Millennials.

The Brief

"This was a design task I completed: creating a mobile-centric bank in the vein of "challenger banks" aimed at millennials."

Planning & UX Deliverables

I was tasked with creating a number of user flows for the usual bank-related activities: onboarding, adding new recipients, getting online support etc.

Outside of the mandatory deliverables I also explored the information architecture and menu structure, user journeys and emotion maps.

The Final Product

The other deliverable requested was a high-fidelity mockup of the primary screen. This was the result of market research, sketching and iteration.

This is the finished product - one is a regular design, the other is a more brutalist two-tone concept designed for those with bad eyesight - a more contrasted concept. Created in Adobe XD, assets built in Photoshop and Illustrator.
